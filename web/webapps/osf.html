Wide Open

2k3 Technologies strongly supports Open Source Software and feels that while proprietary solutions have their appropriate uses, open source solutions provide a viable alternative to proprietary solutions. In terms of the cost-reduction benefits of software development and the promotion of competing technologies, it's a "win-win" situation.

Are you uncomfortable with the idea of your operations becoming too entrenched with a particular software vendor? Are you stuck in the quagmires of the seemingly endless cycles of licensing renewals and upgrades associated with proprietary solutions? If you are and you're not familiar with the Open Source Software movement, you may be surprised by the number of available alternative solutions. You can learn more by visiting one of the many online open source software resources. Here are a couple to get you started:
  The Open Source Initiative
  oSalt.com
  Open Source for Intel
  Open Source Mac
