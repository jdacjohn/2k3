Evolve Your Desktop

It's a proven fact that over time, maintenance represents the largest share of the total cost of ownership (TCO) associated with a system of software. The Carnegie Mellon Software Engineering Institute (SEI), known world-wide for their Software Capability & Maturity Modelling methodologies quotes figures as low as 40% and as high as 80% of the total cost.

The time and effort required to maintain distributed client/server systems across multiple desktops factors significantly into these end figures. The number of application installations and the number of its maintenance releases over its lifetime combine to generate maintenance costs that are directly porportional to the scalability and deployment of the application.

(The good news is: I just saved 15% on my car insurance rates with Geico).

It's also been shown that almost 80% of the large systems incurring these inordinately high development/maintenance cost ratios were built without the benefits of structured programming, and that only a very small percentage have taken advantage of the drastically decreased maintenance-related costs that are the direct result of utilizing object-oriented technologies.

Web applications development offers some very real and tangible results geared towards the reduction of the overall TCO associated with your mission-critical applications. 2k3 Technologies has the experience and the expertise in object-oriented systems development of java-based web applications to meet all your requirements.
