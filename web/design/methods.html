Designs • Web Methods • Logos
Designing and building a website is an interactive process. At 2k3 Technologies, we work closely with you from start to finish and beyond to ensure that the end result is a direct reflection of your vision. We use a simple straight-forward process that is described in the sections below.
  • Web Site Requirements Analysis and Design
  • Web Site Implementation
  • Domain Name & Site Hosting
  • Web Site Maintenance

Are you ready? Contact us now to learn more or get started!

Web Site Requirements Analysis & Design [ back to top ]
Our designers will work with you to determine the 'look and feel' of your site, using a process of continuous feedback from you and refinement to identify the page layouts, color scheme, image utilization, and interactive components, if any. During the design process we will assess the need for custom graphics designs, such as logo development and branding, as well as any digital photography requirements which would enhance the appeal of your site. Content is identified and the navigational shape of the website will begin to emerge.
The results of the design process will be a conceptual site map, showing the structure and form of the website as well as conceptual site designs using Adobe Photoshop work products as baselines.

Web Site Implementation [ back to top ]
Upon approval of the final design, the actual implementation of your website will begin. A site skeleton will be built to verify the navigational behavior of the site pages and then site content will be implemented. During implementation, your site will be accessible via our test web servers so you can access it and validate it as it evolves. You will also be able to check on the status of your web site and view any open issues via our Current Projects page which is accessible by authorized client representatives.

Domain Name & Site Hosting [ back to top ]
If it is determined during the inital discussions of your site requirements that you need a domain name for your site, we will secure the domain name for you so that when you're ready to launch your site, everything will be in place for you to do so. We will also arrange the hosting of your site, should you so desire, through one of our preferred partner providers.

Web Site Maintenance [ back to top ]
After your site is launched and running World Wide Web, we will, at your discretion, provide site maintenance services. Site maintenance is an important aspect of owning a website and is geared towards ensuring that the content on your site is accurate, fresh and up-to-date - all of which are decisive factors in how well your site ranks with major search engines. Maintenance services are also intended to allow your site to grow and change as your business evolves.
